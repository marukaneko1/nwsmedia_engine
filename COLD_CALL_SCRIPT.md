# NWS Media — Cold Call Script

> For outbound calls to leads with **no website** or a **dead/broken website**.
> Call length target: 3–5 minutes. Goal: spark interest, book a discovery call.

---

## Before You Dial (30 seconds)

Have these ready:

- [ ] Business name, category, city
- [ ] Owner/contact first name
- [ ] Google Maps listing open (reviews, rating)
- [ ] Confirm: no website or dead website
- [ ] Top 1–2 competitors in their area who DO have a strong web presence

---

## The Call

### 1. The Intro — Confident, Fast, Human (15 seconds)

**Get past the first 10 seconds. That's the whole game.**

> "Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] with NWS Media — I'll be quick, I know you're busy. I was actually looking at businesses in [city] and came across [Business Name]. You've got [X] reviews at [rating] stars — that's seriously impressive. Quick question — do you have a second?"

**If they say yes → move to Section 2.**

**If they say "what's this about?":**

> "Totally fair. I work with local businesses like yours on their online presence — not just websites, the whole package. I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a site right now / has a site that's down, and I think you're leaving real money on the table. I had a couple ideas — can I take 60 seconds?"

**If they say no / bad time:**

> "No worries at all. When's a better time to catch you for two minutes? I promise it'll be worth it."

*Get the callback time. Log it. Move on.*

---

### 2. The Hook — Make Them Feel the Gap (45 seconds)

**Don't sell yet. Show them what they're missing.**

#### For NO WEBSITE leads:

> "So here's what I noticed — when I Googled '[category] in [city],' your Google Maps listing came up. That's great. But there's nothing to click through to. No website, no portfolio, no way for someone to see your work, check you out, and say 'yeah, these are my guys.' They just scroll to the next business that has one."

> "And right now, your competitors — some of them with fewer reviews and lower ratings than you — they're getting those calls instead. Not because they're better. Just because they showed up online."

#### For DEAD WEBSITE leads:

> "So I actually tried to pull up your website — [website URL] — and it's not loading. It's either down or broken. That means every single person who finds you on Google and clicks your link is hitting a dead page. And Google sees that too — they're actively pushing you down in search results because of it."

> "So you've got this great reputation, [X] reviews, [rating] stars — but the front door to your business online is basically locked."

**Pause. Let that land.** If they react ("yeah I know it's been an issue" / "really?") — acknowledge it and move forward.

---

### 3. The Reframe — This Isn't a Website Pitch (60 seconds)

**This is the most important part. You're not selling a website. You're selling a growth engine.**

> "So here's why I'm calling — I'm not trying to sell you a website. Honestly, anyone can throw up a website. What I'm talking about is completely different."

> "What we do at NWS Media is build a full online presence package. Think of it like this — your business has an incredible reputation on the ground. What we do is make sure your online presence matches that. We're talking:"

> **"One** — a site that's built to rank on Google. Not just look pretty, but actually show up when people search '[category] in [city]' and '[category] near me.' Real SEO, built in from day one."

> **"Two** — a brand experience. When someone lands on your page, it needs to feel premium. Professional photos, clean layout, fast load times. The kind of site where someone looks at it and says 'these guys are legit, I'm calling them right now.'"

> **"Three** — the full marketing foundation. Google ranking, click-to-call, contact forms, your best reviews front and center, mobile optimization — everything working together so your phone rings more. That's the package."

> "Most businesses in your space are either running with no website or something their cousin built five years ago. We put you ahead of all of them."

---

### 4. The Temperature Check (30 seconds)

**Don't push. Read the room.**

> "Is that something that's been on your radar at all — getting your online presence dialed in?"

**If warm ("yeah, I've been meaning to" / "what would that cost?"):**

> "Perfect. What I'd love to do is set up a quick 15-minute call where I walk you through exactly what I'd build for [Business Name] — specific to your business, your market, your competitors. No cost, no commitment. Just a game plan you can look at. Would [day] or [day] work better for you?"

**If lukewarm ("I don't know, I'm pretty busy" / "we get enough work from referrals"):**

> "I hear that, and referrals are great — that tells me your work speaks for itself. But here's the thing — referrals are unpredictable. One slow month and you're scrambling. What we're building is a second pipeline of customers who are actively searching for exactly what you do, right now, in [city]. That's consistent, predictable work coming in on top of your referrals."

> "All I'm asking for is 15 minutes. If it doesn't make sense, I'll tell you that myself. Fair enough?"

**If cold ("not interested" / "I don't need a website"):**

> "I respect that. Let me ask you one thing though — when's the last time you Googled your own business and looked at what a customer sees? Because right now, they're finding your competitors instead of you. If that ever changes and you want to have a conversation, I'm a phone call away. Can I shoot you a quick email with my info just so you have it?"

*Send the email template. Log the lead as cold. Move on.*

---

### 5. Book the Call + Close (30 seconds)

Once they agree to the discovery call:

> "Awesome. I'll send you a quick calendar invite right now so we don't lose it. You'll also get a short email from me before the call with some of the data I pulled on your business — think of it as a free audit."

> "Really appreciate your time, [Name]. Looking forward to showing you what we can do for [Business Name]. Talk soon."

## Objection Handling — Quick Reference

### "How much does this cost?"

> "Depends on what we're building — every business is different. But I'll tell you this: most of our clients make their investment back within the first month or two just from the new customers their site brings in. Let's jump on that 15-minute call and I'll give you a real number based on what [Business Name] actually needs."

*Don't give pricing on the cold call. Get them to the discovery call.*

### "I had a website built before and it didn't do anything."

> "I hear that more than you'd think. And honestly, that's probably because it was just a website — something that looked okay but wasn't built to actually bring in customers. What we do is fundamentally different. SEO, Google ranking, speed, conversion — all engineered from the start. That's why it works."

### "Can't I just use Wix / Squarespace / social media?"

> "You could, and for some businesses that's fine. But those platforms cap out fast when it comes to Google ranking and performance. Google's own data shows template sites score 30–40% lower on speed than custom builds. If ranking on the first page matters to you — and it should because that's where 90% of clicks happen — a custom solution is how you get there."

### "Just send me an email."

> "Absolutely, I'll send that right over. Keep an eye out for it — it'll have some data on your business you probably haven't seen before. And if anything in there makes you go 'huh, I didn't know that' — which it probably will — just reply and we'll set something up. Sound good?"

### "I'm too busy for this."

> "I totally get it — the fact that you're busy tells me business is good, and I respect that. That's exactly why you need this, though. Right now you're the entire sales and marketing team on top of doing the actual work. What we build takes that off your plate — customers find you and call you without you lifting a finger. Let me grab 15 minutes when things slow down. What does [day] look like?"

## Voicemail Script

*If you hit voicemail — leave one. Keep it under 30 seconds.*

> "Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] with NWS Media. I was looking at local [category] businesses in [city] and came across [Business Name] — your reviews are great. I had a couple ideas on how to get your online presence matching the quality of your work. Give me a call back when you get a chance — [phone number]. Again, that's [Your Name], NWS Media, [phone number]. Talk soon."

## Mindset Reminders

- **You're not begging.** You found a problem, you have the solution, and you're giving them a chance to fix it.
- **The first 10 seconds decide everything.** Be warm, be fast, say something specific about their business immediately.
- **Sell the meeting, not the product.** Your only job on this call is to get them to a 15-minute discovery call.
- **Rejection is data, not failure.** A "no" today is a "not yet." Log it, follow up in 30 days, move on.
- **Energy is contagious.** If you sound excited about what NWS Media can do for them, they'll feel it. If you sound like you're reading a script, they'll hang up.
